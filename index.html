Challenge your
team to make small,
sustainable choices
at work.

Greenlist is a simple internal tool that empowers
companies to start a sustainable office lifestyle while
driving change in a way that feels natural. With both a fun
and inspiring approach, Greenlist turns cutting down on
plastic into a friendly in-company competition that
celebrates individuals for their efforts.

Create a cleaner and
better world, on Slack.

Whether it’s choosing reusable food and liquid containers or picking the
vegetarian option for the office lunch, Greenlist allows anyone in your
team to set a positive role model and influence other staff in being more
cautious about plastic pollution and using resources sustainably.

employees

Thanks to a point system based on the
environmental impact of each action, Greenlist
helps you educate your employees on
sustainability while keeping them engaged.
